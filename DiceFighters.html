Dice Fighters is a digital version of a board game I've created. It's an all-out brawler where it's every man for himself. To put it simply, you roll 3 dice, get moves from that depending on the character, and use those moves to beat the other players.

26 Levels!

There are 26 levels in the game which teaches you everything from the rules of the game as well as how to play all characters in the game.

20 unique characters!

A total of 20 different characters to choose from! Almost all character have their own unique attacks as well which makes each character feel different when playing them.

4 different game modes!

There are 4 different game modes in the game. Both the standard game mode and game modes for team play, efficiency, and survivability. There are some other options as well o you can adjust the game to suit your needs.

For 1-6 players

It can be played with any number of players. You can either play it with your friends (recommended) or play on your own against the AI in custom game. Why not mix them together to fill out the gaps? The AI is available in three different levels to suit your need.
