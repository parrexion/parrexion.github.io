The Halarian Ruin is a real-time RPG taking place on a contested planet in the middle of a raging war. The the player will get to control two characters fighting in the war as they try their hardest to survive and win the war.

STORY

The story takes place during a war between two different factions which has been raging for a long time. On one of the contested planets humans have established a base and is trying to survive and fight off the other faction. In order to fight back, they have developed androids to help with fighting the war so each soldier fights together with one of the androids in battle pairs. The story follows one of these battle pairs with the soldier who just lost her android and a new android just booted up for the first time.

GAMEPLAY

The gameplay for the game is similar to a typical RPG. The player will move around in the world talking to people and doing quest. When encountering enemies the battles take place in an area different from the normal world. From battles the player will gain experience and money to buy better equipment.


The difference from most RPGs is that battles are played out using 2 characters simultaneously, the soldier and the android. The android is the main fighter and is controlled with the mouse and shift key. You move around and make gestures with the mouse to do different attacks. From battles you can acquire new attacks to use with the android.


The soldier plays more of a supporting role and is controlled with the keyboard. The soldier does not move but blocks or evades attacks instead. It fights using a laser weapon which can overheat so it is important to take time to cool it down. The soldier can get different kinds of equipment to change up how the weapon works and assist them both in battles.

CHARACTERS

ZAINA

The first main character of the game. A soldier which has fought in the war for some time now so knows most of the things around the base. Had a different android fighting together with her but it got destroyed during a surprise attack by the enemies.

ATSUKO

The other main character of the game. A newly started android which got assigned to Zaina as partner in the battle pair. Built for fighting and does not have much human emotions as all androids. Have yet to learn how everything works and the base and what is going on in the current war.
